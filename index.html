IT'S OUR COTSWOLDS

On 2nd May 2024, voters in the Cotswold District should have the opportunity to vote in a referendum to keep or to replace the 'Leader and Cabinet' system with a modern committee system. The District Council is currently set up so that all decision-making powers are concentrated in the hands of 8 Lib Dem councillors. The other 26 councillors - Lib Dem, Tory, Green, Independent - are excluded. With 3,502 signatures we can force CDC to adopt a more democratic system.

It's Our Cotswolds is an independent group campaigning to raise awareness of the benefits of a collaborative committee system. We're backed by Conservative, Liberal Democrat, Labour and Green supporters - and indeed people who aren't aligned with any party at all.

Chris studied Law at the University of York and takes an interest in constitutional and electoral law.

Chris has ten years' experience as a professional political campaigner and community organiser, winning against both the Labour and Conservative parties in some of the most deprived areas of England.
