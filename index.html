They're our Cotswolds

On 5th May Bristol voters will have the opportunity to vote in a referendum to keep or to replace the mayoral system with a modern committee system.

It's Our City Bristol is an independent group campaigning to raise awareness of the benefits of a collaborative committee system.

In Bristol we elect a mayor once every 4 years to rule over us and make all the decisions.

They have absolute power, very little scrutiny and almost no accountability until 4 years later when we vote again.

We and our elected councillors are mostly locked out of the decision-making.

We think that means our community voices are being ignored.
